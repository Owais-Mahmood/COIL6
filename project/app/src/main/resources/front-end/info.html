Monitoring Sites
Upstream Site Captures baseline water quality above the settlement, before agricultural and residential contamination sources.
Downstream Site Captures cumulative effects of all upstream activity before water reaches the reservoir supply point.
Reservoir Site The most critical sute - monitors water quality at the main community supply reservoir for treatment decisions.
