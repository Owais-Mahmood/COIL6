Stay informed. Respond before it's too late.

The Stroomloop alert system watches over water quality around the clock at three Eastern Cape monitoring sites. The moment a reading crosses a safe threshold, whether pH rises too high, turbidity clouds the water, or conductivity spikes, an alert is triggered and the feed updates in real time.

For communities that rely on these water sources daily, early warning matters. Contaminated water poses real health risks, particularly for children, the elderly, and those with existing conditions. Knowing about a problem early means being able to act: switching sources, boiling water, or contacting local authorities before the situation worsens.
