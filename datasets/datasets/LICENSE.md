## What this material is and is not

These datasets are **synthetic** — generated by a calibrated model, not
collected from real communities. They are grounded in real weather data
(NASA POWER MERRA-2, public domain) and calibrated against published
environmental standards (SANS 241:2015, FAO Papers 56 & 61, and others
cited in the documentation). The causal structures, distributions, and
alert thresholds are documented in full.

The communities represented in the framing of this work — small-scale
farmers, livestock keepers, water users, and neighbourhood residents in
the Eastern Cape, South Africa — are not data subjects. Their contexts
are the motivation for this work, not its raw material. The DATA Project
is committed to community technological agency, not extraction.
